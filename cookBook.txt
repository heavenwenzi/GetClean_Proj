		DATA DICTIONARY - UCI HAR Dataset

SubjectID		
	Subject ID for who performed the activity. 
		1 .. 30
	
ActivityID
	Names of the activies
		WALKING
		WALKING_UPSTAIRS
		WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
		SITTING
		STANDING
		LAYING
	
features
	List of all features that associated with the mean / standard deviation 
	
mean
	Takes mean of each features for each activities
		-1 .. 1
